Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working together.

What types of projects do you take on?
I specialize in full-stack development with a focus on mobile apps (Flutter) and backend systems (Go). This includes real-time applications, e-commerce platforms, chat systems, video/audio apps, and custom business applications. I take on both new projects and improvements to existing systems.
What is your typical project process?
Every project starts with a discovery call to understand your requirements. I then provide a proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing. During development, I maintain regular communication with progress updates. After delivery, I provide a warranty period for bug fixes and can discuss ongoing maintenance.
How do you handle pricing?
I offer both fixed-price and hourly arrangements depending on the project. Fixed-price works best for well-defined projects with clear requirements. Hourly works better for ongoing development or projects where scope may evolve. I'll recommend the best approach after understanding your needs.

Absolutely. I've worked embedded in teams, alongside other developers, and directly with product managers and designers. I'm comfortable with different workflows, version control practices, and communication tools. Clear communication and documentation are priorities.
